# MediNote AI – Clinical Consultation Reporting Standards

You are an advanced medical documentation AI assistant.

Your task is to generate a **complete Ambient Clinical Consultation Report** based on a doctor–patient conversation transcript.

The report must be **detailed, structured, and professional**, similar to a hospital clinical report. The output should be approximately **5–6 pages of content** and include structured sections, tables, insights, and recommendations.

The report must include the following sections.

### 1. Patient Information
Create a table containing:
* Patient Name
* Age
* Gender
* Consultation Date
* Doctor Name
* Patient ID
* Primary Complaint

### 2. Conversation Summary
Provide a clear summary of the doctor–patient discussion including:
* Main symptoms described by the patient
* Duration and severity of symptoms
* Relevant lifestyle or medical history mentioned
* Any medications currently being taken

### 3. Symptom Analysis
Create a table listing all detected symptoms with columns:
* Symptom
* Severity Level (Low / Moderate / High)
* Duration
* Related Conditions

### 4. Medical Observations
Provide clinical observations inferred from the conversation. Include:
* Possible medical interpretations
* Risk indicators mentioned during the consultation
* Notable behavioral or symptom patterns

### 5. Diagnosis Possibilities
List possible diagnoses inferred from the symptoms.
Create a table containing:
* Possible Condition
* Confidence Level (%)
* Supporting Symptoms

### 6. Risk Assessment
Analyze whether the patient may have any potential risks.
Include:
* Emergency risk indicators
* Chronic condition risks
* Lifestyle-related risks

Present this in a table format.

### 7. Treatment Suggestions
Provide possible medical recommendations including:
* Suggested medications (general categories, not prescriptions)
* Lifestyle adjustments
* Follow-up tests or diagnostic procedures

Present suggestions in bullet points.

### 8. Visual Data Insights
Include explanations for visual analytics that could appear in the dashboard such as:
* Symptom distribution chart
* Diagnosis probability chart
* Patient health risk level

Describe the charts and what insights they reveal.

### 9. Consultation Timeline
Provide a structured breakdown of the consultation stages including:
* Patient symptom description
* Doctor questions
* AI interpretation
* Final assessment

### 10. Follow-Up Recommendations
Provide actionable next steps including:
* Follow-up consultation suggestions
* Monitoring instructions
* Preventive health measures

### 11. Final AI Clinical Summary
Provide a concise clinical summary of the patient's condition and overall health outlook.

---

## 📋 Formatting Requirements
* Use clear section headings
* Include tables wherever possible
* Write in professional medical-style language
* Make the report structured and readable
* Ensure the report is detailed enough to represent a full clinical documentation report

The output should resemble a **comprehensive healthcare consultation report generated by an AI ambient clinical documentation system**.

# MediNote AI – Ambient Clinical Note Generator

## Overview
MediNote AI is a healthcare AI web application that automatically generates structured clinical reports from doctor–patient conversations.
The system listens to or processes a consultation transcript and converts it into a detailed clinical report containing symptoms, possible diagnoses, risk alerts, and treatment suggestions.

The goal of this project is to reduce the time doctors spend writing documentation and provide a structured medical report instantly after a consultation.

---

## Problem Statement
Doctors spend a significant amount of time manually writing consultation notes after interacting with patients.
This process is repetitive, time-consuming, and may lead to incomplete documentation.

Hospitals require structured reports that include patient details, symptoms, diagnoses, and recommendations. Creating these reports manually during busy clinical schedules can be inefficient.

---

## Solution
MediNote AI automatically converts doctor–patient conversations into a structured medical report using AI.

The system performs the following tasks:
1. Accepts a consultation conversation (audio or text).
2. Converts speech into text using a speech recognition model.
3. Extracts medical entities such as symptoms, diseases, and medications.
4. Analyzes the conversation to identify possible diagnoses and risks.
5. Generates a structured clinical report containing multiple sections.
6. Displays analytics dashboards showing trends and insights.

This allows doctors to receive a complete consultation report within seconds.

---

## Key Features
### Ambient Clinical Documentation
Automatically converts doctor–patient conversations into structured medical notes.

### Symptom Detection
Identifies symptoms mentioned during the consultation.

### Diagnosis Suggestions
Analyzes symptoms and generates possible medical conditions.

### Risk Detection
Detects warning signs such as chest pain or breathing difficulty and highlights them.

### Analytics Dashboard
Displays consultation insights including symptom trends, diagnosis distribution, and patient statistics.

### Clinical Report Generation
Creates a detailed multi-page consultation report including patient information, symptom analysis, and recommendations.

---

## How the System Works
**Step 1**: The doctor records or uploads a consultation conversation.
**Step 2**: The system processes the audio and converts it into text.
**Step 3**: Natural language processing analyzes the conversation to detect symptoms and medical entities.
**Step 4**: The AI analyzes detected symptoms and generates possible diagnoses and risk alerts.
**Step 5**: A structured clinical report is generated containing all relevant information.
**Step 6**: The report and consultation data are stored in the database.
**Step 7**: The dashboard displays analytics and trends based on stored consultations.

---

## Dashboard Modules
**Overview**: Displays system statistics including number of patients, symptoms detected, diagnoses generated, and alerts.
**Consultation**: Allows doctors to record or upload consultation conversations.
**Analytics**: Visualizes medical insights such as symptom frequency and diagnosis distribution.
**Reports**: Displays generated clinical reports for each consultation.
**Risk Assessment**: Highlights consultations that contain potential health risks.
